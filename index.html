I am a recent graduate from TU Darmstadt, where I studied Computational Engineering.

At TU Darmstadt I was a student research assistant at the Visual Inference Lab of Prof. Stefan Roth. Furthermore, I was a working student and wrote my master thesis at the Image Understanding Group of Mercedes-Benz led by Dr. Uwe Franke and Dr. Marius Cordts. Moreover, I was involved in a research project at the EPFL VITA Lab of Prof. Alexandre Alahi. Recently, I conducted a research project at ETH Zurich working in the Computer Vision Lab of Prof. Luc Van Gool. Currently, I am an intern at Stanford University as part of the Geometric Computation Group of Prof. Leonidas Guibas.
